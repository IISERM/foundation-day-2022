Quiz

Come rack your brains and hone your puzzle cracking skills with the open quiz as well as one meant specifically for school students. We dont prescribe a syllabus, just an open and curious mind. You can either make it to finals and compete or sit in the audience and still learn new things. There are also exciting prizes to be won!

Teams of 3 members (5th to 12th standard students)

Demonstrations

Ever wondered how all those fancy experiments work? Wanted to see biological specimens up close? Play around with chemicals in a safe manner? Learn mathematical tricks? Watch gravity-defying physics experiments? This is your chance! You get to not only see these things in the research labs, you also get the chance to interact one-on-one with the students and scholars working in this fields!

Treasure Hunt

Are you fascinated by hidden treasures? Do you think you are good at cracking riddles? Want to bring out the adventurer in you? Then, this is the place to be. Connect the dots and the treasure will be yours! Come play in teams and you will gain scientific insights along with exciting prizes.

Teams of 4-5 members (5th to 10th standard students only)

Mystery Solving
