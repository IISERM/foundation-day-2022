Who are we?

Vision

Indian Institute of Science Education and Research Mohali envisions a new generation of scientists dedicated to the pursuit of knowledge in frontier areas of basic sciences.

Mission

As a centre of academic excellence, IISER Mohali has as its mission the imparting of quality science education and the inculcating of the spirit of research through innovative teaching and research methodologies.

Legacy

Every year IISER Mohali celebrates its foundation day. We invite school kids from around the city to explore the science. They get the opportunity to learn science and have fun along the way.
